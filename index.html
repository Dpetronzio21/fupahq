Football Universe Parlay Association: 101

Each week, 11 people chip in $10 and bet an 11-team $110 parlay.

$110 (11) team parlay - payouts should be in the $100-200k range, $5k-10k payout per person after tax.

BETTING GUIDELINES – we want to win some real money!!

  • NO MONEY LINE FAVORITES THAT ARE BELOW -115
  • SPREADS CAN BE UP TO -125
  • NO PROPS THAT ARE BELOW -115

This is how we do that:

Parlay Winners/Losers

There are 198 total picks per season (11 x 18). Each parlay pick that is lost will cost you $15, each parlay win will be worth an equal “share” of the money paid by the losers.

So for example, if we win 100 of the 198 possible games (59.5%), the payout per parlay win would be $14.7 per game. I will track this and run a tab during the season.

RELEGATED MEMBERS

Relegated members will still make picks to show a history of win %, we will upgrade an associate member into one of the 11 full-member spots once their win % is in the top 11.

SEASON CHAMPIONSHIP

We also want to remain competitive throughout and reward those who have shown continuous success over the entire season. We do an optional season long side-pot of $50 per person.

At the end of the year, we will split that pot amongst every FUPA member that has picked above 60%, we call it The 600 club. In sports gambling, to break even, you must win 60% of your bets (to overcome a standard 10% juice) anything less than that and you are a loser.

We split the winnings in this fashion

For example, 4 members were all above .600 last season (in 2023 there were only 2)

  • Player 1: 15 wins (15 wins / 49 total wins = 30% of the pot)
  • Player 2: 12 wins (12 wins / 49 total wins = 24.5% of the pot)
  • Player 3: 11 wins (11 wins / 49 total wins = 22.45% of the pot)
  • Player 4: 11 wins (11 wins / 49 total wins = 22.45% of the pot)

We also award the #1 FUPA member with a giant trophy in their Honor
