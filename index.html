How FUPA Works

  • $10 each per week paid to me for the cost of the parlay.
  • Venmo: @Dario-Petronzio
  • $110 (11) team parlay - payouts should be in the $100-150k range, $5k-10k payouts after tax.

But the odds of us winning a huge parlay are small, so we must find a way to punish the bad picks and reward the good picks. Here's how we do that:

Weekly Parlay Winners/Losers

  • If you win your pick, you are guaranteed to win at least $10 (breakeven for the week).
  • If you lose your pick, you are guaranteed to lose at least $10.

Scenarios

  • If >50% of the parlay wins, e.g., 6/11 are winners, the losers must pay enough to ensure all winners get $10. (5 losers would owe $12 each to equal $60 for the winners.)
  • If >50% of the parlay loses, e.g., only 2 players are winners, they get to split the total of the losers' $10 each. (9 losing players each paying $10 = $90 split between two winners = $45 each.)
  • If it's a draw, e.g., 5/10 teams win, losers' money goes straight to winners.
  • Worst Case Scenario: You are the only person who did not get their pick right and jeopardized our big payout; you now owe every other player $10.
  • Best Case Scenario: We win the parlay and split it evenly amongst winners.
  • 2nd Best Case Scenario: You are the only person who picked correctly; you get $10 from every other player.
  • In the case of a push and a parlay win, the player that pushed gets half a share; the remaining half is distributed amongst the winners.
  • Potential Amendment: There are 198 total picks per season; each parlay pick that is lost will be -$15, each parlay win will be worth an equal “share” of the money paid by the losers.

Betting Guidelines

  • NO money line favorites that are -115 or higher.
  • Spreads can be up to -120.
  • NO props that are -115 or higher.

Relegation

Using the last 5 weeks, we calculate the win % of each player; the top 11 win % players are in the parlay, the rest are relegated.

  • Tiebreakers:
    1. Odds value of the last 5 picks.
    2. Last 3-week win %.
    3. Odds value of the last 3 picks.

Associate Members

Associate members/relegated members will still make picks to show a history of win %; we will upgrade an associate member into one of the 11 full-member spots once their win % is in the top 11.

Season Championship

  • $50 per person (full or associate members can both play, this is optional).
  • 2024 payouts:
    • 1st place: $500
    • 2nd place: $175
    • 3rd place: $75
  • Potential Amendment - THE 600 CLUB: $50 per person (full or associate members can both play, this is optional).
  • 2025 Payouts: All members who win above 60% of their picks will share the total pot based on how many wins the member has. For example, the following 4 members all pick above .600:
    • Player 1: 15 wins (15 wins / 49 total wins = 30%)
    • Player 2: 12 wins (12 wins / 49 total wins = 24.5%)
    • Player 3: 11 wins (11 wins / 49 total wins = 22.45%)
    • Player 4: 11 wins (11 wins / 49 total wins = 22.45%)
